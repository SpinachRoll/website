Smithing Calculators

To use this calculator, put in your current level or XP, and the level you'd like to get to. Pick if you'd like to calculator for iron or steel smelting. You can also click the "Show/Hide Stock" button, add the ore or bars that you currently own, and it will subtract those from the total amount you still need. If you change anything, hit "Calculate" again to get the fresh info.
